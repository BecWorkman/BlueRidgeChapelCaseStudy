Maggie Valley is a ski town/tourist destination with skiing, great restaurants, horseback riding, hiking, live music, one of the biggest historical motorcycle museums in the country, and so much more!

BEDROOMS

This cabin is perfect for a romantic retreat, small family vacation, golf getaway, or friend's trip. Its two private bedrooms are designed for rest and relaxation with comfortable mattresses and high-end linens. The master bedroom boasts a king bed while the other room features a Queen. Soft carpeting, handcrafted furniture, and windows that allow for plenty of natural light should help you settle in and feel at home in both rooms. The Futon in the basement and the Sleeper Sofa in the lounge offer additional sleeping options should you need them.

BATHROOMS

The master bedroom offers a private full bathroom, complete with a walk-in shower plus a built-in shower bench behind frosted glass doors. The bathroom also has a mirrored vanity, ample storage, and hotel-quality towels. The second bathroom provides a tub/shower combo that's perfect for a soak after a day out skiing in Maggie Valley.

KITCHEN

The open-concept kitchen combines the original character and charm of a classic cabin with the amenities of a luxury home. It features exposed wood beams, custom cabinets, and granite countertops. The drawers and cabinets are stocked with utensils, wine glasses, coffee mugs, and kitchen gadgets to help prepare and serve meals for a family. You can store any leftovers in the full-size refrigerator. Early risers can brew coffee in the coffee maker and enjoy it out on the balcony, and night owls can heat up leftovers or pop some corn in the microwave and enjoy it while watching a movie in the living room. A breakfast bar with high-top seating provides a convenient place to eat breakfast before heading out to explore the mountains.The formal dining area sits in an alcove of windows overlooking the balcony. This space is perfect for enjoying a family-style meal together or opening up your laptops to get some work done before heading out on a family hike. The dining table has seating for six and offers great views of the natural beauty right outside.

LOUNGE

The living room is surrounded by windows and fills with sunshine in the afternoons. The soaring ceilings, hardwood floors, antique furniture, and modern finishes create a space the whole family will love. It features a gorgeous stone fireplace, a flat-screen TV, and comfortable recliners. A secondary hangout space in the basement features a pool table, futon, and dedicated workspace. This area is surrounded by windows overlooking the mountainside and can be used as a private office or an additional bedroom. Glass doors lead from this room out to the balcony, where you can sit in rocking chairs and enjoy the view.

OUTSIDE SPACE

The Waynesville and Maggie Valley areas are known for their stunning natural beauty, and staying a week or two in this home allows you to experience it for yourself without ever having to venture far.The sweeping shady balconies with classic rocking chairs are the perfect place for enjoying outdoor living. There's an electric grill for BBQ to have outside on the deck with the family. Enjoy conversations in the evenings around the fire pit in the big backyard.

GUEST ACCESS

When you stay in this beautiful cabin, you have the entire place to yourself. You can use all the rooms and amenities. The home has a washer and dryer, baby equipment, a barbecue grill, laptop-friendly workspaces, and fully equipped kitchen and bathroom essentials that you are free to use. You can park right on site. AWD or 4WD is recommended during the winter months - you will get stuck otherwise. This is a mountain with steep roads and sharp switchbacks. Utah Mountain Road is a paved mountain road and the only paved mountain road the entire way up so please take that route only. Please note the owner does not have an AWD or 4WD vehicle and commutes up and down the mountain several times a week. AWD and 4WD drive is not required during the summer months but is a plus if you have it to make the drive more comfortable. You will be given a unique key code to access the keypad on the front door and all the information about connecting to the internet before you arrive. From here, you're just minutes from local grocery stores, great restaurants, hiking trails, parks and playgrounds, and some of the most beautiful natural landmarks and views in the country.
