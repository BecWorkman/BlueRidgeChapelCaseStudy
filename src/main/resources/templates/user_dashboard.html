Where You'll Be:

Waynesville, North Carolina, United States

Utah Mountain Estates is a private mountain community. The community is very friendly with many places to walk and soak in the Appalachian views. Nestled in the mountains between Waynesville, NC, and Maggie Valley, NC. Historic Downtown Waynesville is an artsy mountain town with plenty of shops and great food all within a short walk of each other. Maggie Valley is a ski town/tourist destination with skiing, great restaurants, horseback riding, hiking, live music, one of the biggest historical motorcycle museums in the country, and so much more! Being as Utah Mountain Estates is a private community guests are expected to respect the neighbors and their property. Parties, loud noises, and unruly behavior are absolutely prohibited.
